About Me

"Hey there! My name is M Taufik Fadillah and I'm a student at the State Islamic University of North Sumatra. I'm a front-end developer with a passion for creating great websites and apps. This portfolio website is all about showing off my skills and projects I've worked on. I've got a knack for UI/UX design, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and I love using these tools to bring my ideas to life. I'm always looking to push the boundaries and create unique and intuitive user experiences. Take a look around and you'll see some of the amazing things I've built so far. From clean and simple design to complex interactions, I've got a wide range of skills to showcase. So, if you're in need of a front-end developer or just want to chat about tech, feel free to get in touch with me. I'd love to connect and learn from you too!"

Let's be friends.

"Let's be friends and share ideas! In the tech and programming world, collaboration and sharing knowledge is key to growing and solving problems. Through this website, we hope to establish great connections and be beneficial to each other. If you have any questions or just want to chat about tech, don't hesitate to reach out. Let's learn and grow together!"
